# Privacy Policy — 2exp11

_Last updated: 2026-06-23_

**2exp11** collects no personal data, shows no advertising, and uses no analytics or
tracking. Nothing is transmitted to us or to any third party. All game data (your
current game, best score and settings) is stored locally on your device only.

## Optional: Google Play Games

If you choose to sign in to Google Play Games leaderboards, sign-in and score
submission are handled by Google Play Games Services and are governed by
[Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). The app itself
neither stores nor shares this information. Leaderboards are entirely optional and
the game is fully playable without signing in.

## Permissions

The app requests the `VIBRATE` permission solely to provide haptic feedback on
moves. It is used on-device only and sends nothing anywhere.
