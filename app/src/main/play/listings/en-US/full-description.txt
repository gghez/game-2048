2exp11 is a tile-merging puzzle game: swipe, combine matching numbers and try to reach the 2¹¹ (2048) tile.

Simple rules, instant pick-up-and-play, and a challenge that builds with every merge. Perfect for a quick round or a long, thoughtful session.

A REAL GIFT, FOREVER
• No advertising
• No in-app purchases
• No tracking, no data collection
• Free and open source (MIT license)

FEATURES
• Smooth sliding and polished tile animations
• Score and best score kept on the device
• "Undo" button to take back your last move
• Light or dark theme, your choice
• Optional vibration and sound on every move
• Optional Google Play leaderboards (never required)

2exp11 collects no personal data and sends nothing to third parties. Everything stays on your device.

Enjoy!
